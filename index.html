Award Recognition

Breaking New Ground Award

Honoring real-time radar-based nowcasting innovation for Singapore's tropical weather.

By Htet Naing

Real-time rainfall nowcasting

Bridging AI research with operational meteorology

I am thrilled to share that I have received the Breaking New Ground Award for my work in developing and deploying an operational, real-time, radar-based rainfall nowcasting pipeline encompassing a deep learning-based nowcasting model.

This recognition highlights the progress made in bridging AI research with real-world meteorological applications, enabling more reliable and accurate rainfall predictions in Singapore's tropical weather.

I am very grateful for the support from my colleagues and collaborators who are part of this journey. This award acknowledges not just a personal milestone, but a collective stride toward the future of AI in meteorology.

01

Operational pipeline

Real-time radar rainfall nowcasting built for operational water management decisions for Singapore's National Water Agency (PUB).

02

Deep learning model

A nowcasting model designed to turn radar observations into near-term guidance.

03

Applied AI impact

Research translated into meteorological tools for Singapore's fast-changing rain.
